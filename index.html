Noise Controller Features

Noise Controller is a fun way to help you reward good classroom behavior and prepare children for a lifetime of learning. With Noise Controller, you can:

  • Set the noise level you want to keep..
  • Choose the timing of when new animals show up.
  • Use the app to track multiple classes, and track their progress.
